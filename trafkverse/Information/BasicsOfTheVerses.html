What are Verses

A Verse is a place where something can exist inside of it. A verse does not have a predefined shape, every verse is unique is some way or form. They can come into existence and go out of existence if they are not stable enough.

Where are they?

Everything exists in one of the many verses. And all of the Verses exist, they are the most outer thing there is, they are not in anyting, there is no outside, they are the outside.

How do they come into existence

You know the quantum field? Every Verse has one. The All has a only positive field, the None a pure negative one and Kosmos has a positiv and negativ mix. Outside of the Verse, it is kinda the same, sometimes verses pop in and out of existence and only the stable enough ones stay.

The All is a place where all positive, normal Matter exists. The All is infinitly small, it has no size. From the inside, everything exists in it. it is EVERYTHING. Antimatter / Anam cannot persist, because it is a positive only place. All is/was a illogical place soly because it does not have a logical size.

The All is also the Main Base for Mana, wich has transformed the All into its terretory and place for planning, because it was the only Verse where it could exist.

In the Beginning the All was also infinitely big from the inside and infinitely small from the outside, because it had a natural Attribute: False size. Then The Mana came and made the All not infinitely big, only expanding/contracting when needed. The Attributes remained.

The None is an infinitely big place, where only antimatter / Anam can exist and positive matter just gets erased by the particles of the quantum field of a fully anti verse. None is a illogical place soly because it does not have a logical size.
