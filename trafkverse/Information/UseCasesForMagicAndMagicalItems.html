Magic Wands

Normally you cast a spell from your Fingers. Now there are also so called Wands, that can be used to cast magic more efficiently. Those Wands are made from some Wood and are carved into the desired shape. Then they are enchanted wich some Attributes that use some of the holders Mana to store it in the Wand, where the Mana beginns to multiply, saving the User some of their Mana. Depending on the power of the attributes or size of the wand, the effect can be greater. A Staff is a combination of a wand and a cane. Usually also more powerful.

Potions

POtions are drinks enchanted with Mana Attributes that give the user effects for a period of time. They are created with the special attribute: soulactivated(1.05x). This only makes the spell active, once a being with a soul comes into contact with the enchanted matter. This also works with food and other things. The usual usecase fpr them is in the healing aspect. Many different and costly attributes can be used to heal oneself and because they are so expensive, most often they only come to use in potions, that are produced by special companies that posess some kind of Urlstein.

MagicCircles

A Magic circle is a spell, that is carved into one connected piece of matter (like a grass field that it disconnected via a small dirtline carved into the grass via a stick) and is activated by soulactivated, when a being steps on it. This is used for trapps, but also very long and complicated spells that no being can manage to visualize (then instead of soulactivated, they use soulactivated+, wich only works when the being wants to activate it.). The Mana needed for the spell has to be absobed into the matter first, because the circle takes only the Mana from the matter it is incarved in. Magicians can refill the matter, once the spell has been fired. The circle also uses all of the mana, not a specified amount, altough some circles have an attribute for that aswell.

Urlstones

Urlstones are stones from the first Realm Ursel, where they are still sometimes mined to this day (it is forbidden to pick one up for themselfs). The stones were created by Mana itself and therefore posess emense potetial storage for Mana. Because they are also almost 400 Bibbion Years old, they absorbed Many hundreds of Millions of LE. Inside of the stone, Mana multiplies and because the stones already reached their maximum capacity, the mana flows out of it, creating a faint glow. They are typicly used in production and the gods also use them to keep their worlds alive and well. Typicly every world has one Urlstone somewhere in their world and the corperations, depending on how big they are, also get one (if they need one ofc).
