#### picture description of: Ursel before Alfonz reform
A 50m by 35m by 2m big floating isle in a almost pitch black, purple blue void. Its upper side if flat, a flat grass disk with 0,85m of dirt underneath.
Its lower side is a streched half sphere. It is a thin, oval shape and made of some kind of limestone, a rather soft stone and smooth, to be specific.
There are 2 small oak like trees, one on the upper left corner and one on the middle right edge, they are both fresh and healthy. The ground is mostly grass, but there are some smaller glassless dirt/mud patches. There are a few flowers and insects, but nothing noteworthy. For Example there are some dandelions, daisies, earthworm and rabbit. But there are also a few other things to see.
There is no sun, only the glow of mana, wich are yellow white balls of light that float around the isle, wich is enough to see, but still darker than you would like.
It is also surrounded by a transparent, newly forming violet RealmTrafkBubble. The bubble is enormous, so large that the entire isle looks tiny in comparison, and it keeps a great distance from the island. It is very thin and nearly see through. It is far from complete, as almost none of its surface exists yet. Instead, only small, scattered fragments of it are present, each piece floating in place as if to outline where the finished bubble will eventually be. These pieces are spread out and disconnected, like many ripped cloth scraps suspended in the air, held loosely in the rough shape of a sphere by nothing more than chance. There is only one bubble, though its broken, sparse state makes it look more like a ghost of a bubble than a real one.
