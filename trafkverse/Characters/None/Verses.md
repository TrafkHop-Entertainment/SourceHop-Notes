# The Child of ZK (Das Kind der Zauberkraft)

## History
### 1. The Child (399b999m985k bc)
The Child of All, a being with the connection to the All, but devoid of any prior memories, its being only formed by them. It was a hyperintelligent being, smarter than anything else, it was perfect, the ultimate being. Wings to fly, Claws with 5 Fingers to attack and grab, a tail to keep its balance, ... and the mind of the greatest and kindest of possible minds. It's psychic however was rather fragile for its purpose, thats because the Child could hear the ALL, wich was always thinking something, be it kind or horrifying. That took a stroll on the being.
So the Child began to find a way to cap the connection to the ALL. It began with researching everything about ZK and ZT. After it was allknowing, knowing almost as much as the ALL, it came to the realisation, that only one thing could be done.
So it made a decision that was not in the ALL's favour. It itself created a "clone" of itself, just with less features and memories AND most importantly, no psychic to the ALL. It named him Jaßwäh!
